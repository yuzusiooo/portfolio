Unreal Engine 4 Multiplayer Demo

Advanced Games Programming, University of Technology Sydney (2023)

Unreal Engine 4 Multiplayer demo, where 2 players fight using drones in a randomly generated asteroid field.

About this project

I made this as the final project for the Advanced Games Programming subject, where I and another classmate made a demo that had procedural and multiplayer element.

This subject was overall fairly difficult since this was the first time I have used both Unreal Engine 4 and C++, but the demo worked the way we intended, and I was able to understand how the multiplayer component works and are able to make use of it in future projects.
