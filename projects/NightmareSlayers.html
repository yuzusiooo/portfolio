Nightmare Slayers

Game Design Studio, University of Technology Sydney (2023)

Nightmare Slayers is a Third Person Shooter where you play as 3 different characters, each with unique playstyles.
Fly through the dream world as the Nightmare Slayers, use your weapons and abilities to shoot, dodge and defeat the demon horde.

About this project

This game was made for the Game Design Studio groupwork task, where I, and 3 others, were tasked to design and develop a game in 8 weeks. We would work on the game in a daily basis, and every 2 weeks a playtest session is done where we analyse the feedbacks and assess future plans.

This is one of my favourite projects that I have worked on, since our group was always communicating and working on the game, and it really felt like a collaborative effort that paid off very well in the end.
We put a lot of effort into the shooting and flying actions, and we were able to get consistent feedbacks that our game was unique, polished and fun to play.

We were able to attend to a student project showcase event at our university, and our game was played by various students and guests from the industry.
Given more time, I think we could have fixed some balance issues and polish the atmosphere more, but I am happy that I was able to be part of this game.
